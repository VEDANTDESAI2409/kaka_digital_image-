Kaka Digital Image ERP aims to become a complete digital platform for photography studios.

- Booking Management

- Media Management
- AI Assisted Organization
- Client Galleries
- Payments
- Reports
- Mobile Applications
- Cloud Storage
- Analytics
- Multi Studio Support
